Answers.

Reach out to Ezekiel directly for anything else.

How do I earn a certificate?+
Complete all 5 lessons in a course, then take the 5-question test. Score 3 or more to pass. Once you pass, a certificate generator appears with your name and the course title — signed by Ezekiel Afetor. You can download it as a PNG image.
What if I fail the test?+
Go back, re-read the lessons you struggled with, and try again. There's no limit on retries. The process of revisiting the material is part of the learning — just like real school.
How do I access a book summary?+
5 of the 9 book summaries are completely free — just tap any free book and it opens immediately. The other 4 (The Richest Man in Babylon, Think and Grow Rich, The Total Money Makeover, and I Will Teach You to Be Rich) are available to Premium members.
Do I need an account?+
You can explore courses without an account. But signing up (free) lets you track your progress across all courses and saves your name to your certificates. It takes under 30 seconds to create an account.
Who is Ezekiel Afetor?+
Ezekiel Afetor is the founder of The Real School. He created this platform after realising that formal education consistently fails to equip people worldwide with the practical financial, social, and life skills that determine real outcomes.
Is my data safe?+
Your account information is stored securely for this session. We only collect your name and email for account purposes. We never share your information with third parties.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

The Real School ("the App", "we", "us") is a free life skills education platform. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in plain, readable form.

Usage Information: We store your course progress, quiz scores, certificates earned, and books you've unlocked or read, so you can pick up where you left off.

Communications: If you use our messaging features (direct messages, the community chat), we store the content of those messages and the names/emails of the people involved, so conversations can be displayed to you.

Optional Information: If you submit a review or testimonial, we store your name, email, star rating, and review text. If you were referred by another user, we record that relationship to support our referral program.

Device Information: If you enable push notifications, we store a device-specific subscription token so we can deliver notifications to you. This token does not identify you personally beyond your account.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain your account and course progress
  • Generate and display certificates you've earned
  • Enable messaging and community features between users
  • Send you a one-time welcome email when you sign up
  • Send push notifications you've opted into (e.g. new messages, certificates earned)
  • Display anonymized or aggregate statistics (e.g. total student count)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not show advertising in the app.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Supabase, for secure database storage of your account and app data
  • EmailJS, solely to send a one-time welcome email after signup
  • Netlify, to host the application

Each of these providers may process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security practices.

Data Sharing With Other Users

Your name and email may be visible to other users you interact with directly — for example, in direct messages, friend requests, or community chat. Your profile and earned certificates are only visible to users you have accepted as friends.

Data Retention and Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from Settings. Deleting your account removes your stored personal data from our systems. Messages you've sent to other users may remain visible to the recipient even after account deletion, consistent with how messaging works on most platforms.

Children's Privacy

The Real School is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
